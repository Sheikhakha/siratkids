Tawheed Curriculum منهج العقيدة

Unit 1 : Tawheed Al-Rububiyyah : توحيد الربوبية

1
The Three Principles الأصول الثلاثة Allah is my Lord, Islam is my religion, Muhammad (ﺟ) is my Prophet.
› 2
Allah the Creator الله الخالق Allah created me, all people, animals, birds, the sun, the moon, and everything.
› 3
Allah My Creator الله خالقي Allah is the One who created me, including my hearing and my sight.
› 4
Allah the Owner الله المالك Allah is the Owner of the heavens, the earth, and everything in existence.
› 5
Allah the Provider الله الرزاق There is no provider except Allah. He provides us with food, drink, and sustains all creatures.
›

Unit 2 : Tawheed Al-Uloohiyyah : توحيد الألوهية

1
Why Did Allah Create Us? لماذا خلقنا الله؟ Allah created us solely to worship Him alone (Surah Adh-Dhariyat: 56).
› 2
The Word of Tawheed كلمة التوحيد La ilaha illa Allah — There is no one worthy of worship except Allah.
› 3
I Am a Muslim أنا مسلم I obey Allah my Lord, pray, fast, and call upon Him alone.
› 4
I Love Allah My Lord أحب الله ربي I worship Allah alone without partners and love Him for making me a Muslim.
›

Unit 3 : My Religion is Islam : ديني الإسلام

1
My Religion is Islam ديني الإسلام Islam is the true religion (Surah Ali ’Imran: 19).
› 2
The Two Testimonies الشهادتان Learning the Shahada: La ilaha illa Allah, Muhammadun Rasoolullah.
› 3
Love for the Prophet ﺟ حب النبي ﺟ Loving Prophet Muhammad (ﺟ), the best of mankind.
› 4
Love for the Companions حب الصحابة Loving Abu Bakr, Umar, Uthman, and Ali (radhiyallahu anhum).
›
